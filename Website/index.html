CXR Prediction

Lab testing for COVID-19 (also known as the novel coronavirus or SARS-CoV2) has increased greatly since the virus spread across the globe in spring 2020. Current evidence suggests that chest x-rays are generally NOT specific enough to either diagnose or rule out COVID-19 on their own.

But imaging does have a limited role to play: when used with lab tests, a medical history and a physical exam, x-rays can be helpful for diagnosing COVID-19 or determining the severity of the disease in some patients.
